User Dashboards

Each organization has a separate dashboard for the member and admin. This is the central hub where users will work to quickly find pertinent information about the organization (as well as one-click manage). The dashboard cards can be ordered or hidden by preference of the user. Pay attention to the importance of this feature, because it’s unique in our industry.

With a tool like this who needs setup fees? We don’t charge extra to setup your website, because you can start creating your custom built design today. We’re the best in our industry to offer this kind of customization. Our proprietary builder tool is second to none. The premium quality of the look and feel of our designs are unmistakable. Create web pages to your heart’s content. While some companies limit your site pages, with our proprietary website builder there is no limit to the amount you can have. Use our custom built templates or create your own with our dozens of element widgets to highlight what’s important to your organization.

Responsive Designs

Be comforted in knowing that our designs are first class regardless if viewed on phone, tablet or pc. They scale/auto-adjust based on the viewing device, offering instant customizable flexibility. So you can add your own logos, photos and elements for aesthetically pleasing perfection.

Custom Domain

Password Protection

SSL Certificate

Azure Nightly Backups

Unlimited Storage

Communication

Email Blasts

Choose to send unlimited email alerts to one member, a specified group or all members of your organization, day or night. This feature gives you the flexibility to customize the format of your message, as well as attach a document (such as a newsletter) or image.

View More

Text Alerts

Choose to send text alerts to one member, a specified group or all members of your organization, day or night. This feature is great for getting the attention of your members who don’t often check email.  Find a lost pet, warn about incoming severe weather and much to raise attention to your members almost instantly.

*First 1000 texts are free on the Professional Plan and first 3000 texts are free on the Premium Plan.  Extra text messages can be purchased for a fee of $25.00, per every 1000 text bundles.

Newsfeed

The way of the nested message board has come and gone. Newsfeed allows the members or admin to immediately create topics for discussion. Users can attach images, videos, as well as like, comment and pin posts that matter. This feature was built with today’s social media experience in mind, emojis and all.

Private Chat

Members, Groups, the Board, Committees and Admin can have their own private conversation through the chat feature. This feature allows the creator to find and select who is included in the conversation. It works like text messaging on your phone, so you can attach documents, images and videos direct from your device.

Digest Notifications

Allow members to keep updated on what’s going on in the community, by choosing to receive a weekly or monthly email.  Digest helps your members stay engaged, and alerts them of important items that need attention, to showcasing new events, group discussions, an upcoming payment and so much more.

Newsletter Creation

We have a designer on staff that will tailor your logo, branding, images, colors and text to any size newsletter you desire.  We will populate the newsletter for you, and you can email it out through our software.  We will also provide the file to you for print, so you can mail it if you like.

Financials

Online Payments

Collect payments online on behalf of your organization. Members can easily pay their dues/fees with one click (one-time or recurring). Additionally, if your plan allows for it, members can also pay for event registration and to reserve amenities.

View More

One-Time Charges

One-Time Charges let communities collect money from members to purchase items like lost pool access keys or replacement mailboxes.

View More

Accounting

Bookkeeping made easy, with no accounting degree necessary. Maintain real-time reports (like P&L, Balance Sheet) that can easily be shared with the membership and Board alike. Customizable chart of accounts and simplistic bank reconciliation.

Vendor Management

Manage your vendors by storing items like EIN, contract information, quotes and a history of transactions. This feature is perfect for self-managed associations looking to maintain records on all the companies whom you do business.

View More

Budgeting

Forecast income and expenses through the building of a budget. Use historical data from previous periods to project with accuracy. Look at month over month, or quarter over quarter. Save budgets and download reports for future comparisons.

Print/Stuff/Mail Invoices

Downloadable postal formatted invoices for some or all members; complete with return and send to addresses, as well as built-in fold lines.  We also help guide you on which type of envelope to buy for your printable invoices.  If you would like us to print/stuff/mail on your behalf, rates start at $1.80 letter, with everything included.
Features

Document Repository

With the ability to create unlimited folders and infinite nesting of subfolders, your organization can store crucial documents such as: covenants, meeting minutes, bylaws, etc. The admin also has the option to secure documents for specific access levels and mass download folders direct to their device.

View More

Member Directory

All your members stored in one database. Choose to have a secure directory page where users with access can see all members of the organization, with a pinned map of each address. It should be noted that members have the option to choose to be listed or not in the directory.

View More

Calendar

Let members of your organization know what events are coming up and when. The calendar keeps everyone informed about board meetings, picnics, onsite scheduled maintenance, etc. Keeping members in the know is ideal for engagement.

View More

Contact Form

Allow members of the organization and potential new members to submit a contact form to inquire within. The admin chooses if the form will be sent to one or multiple email addresses for collection of the data.

View More

FAQs

Help members or potential new members find answers to pertinent questions about your organization. Frequently asked questions is formatted in an accordion style, seamless format that allows for optimal interaction.

View More

Households

It became clear to us that there needs to be the ability for members to perform individual actions on the site, but also the need to group actions under one roof.  Households gives organizations the ability to pay dues and vote at the household level, instead of by individual member.  This assures only one vote and/or invoice per address, submitted/payable by any designated member(s) of the household.

Photo Gallery

Highlight photos of your organization, members, amenities or recent events in an aesthetically pleasing format. You have the ability to create specific folder categories, designate a feature image for each folder, as well as load and name the photos within each folder accordingly.

View More View More

Custom Forms

Create unlimited custom forms to allow the collection of data for specific topics within the organization. Whether you want to collect info on violations, arc requests or inquire about opinions, the custom form builder is the dynamic tool you need. Attach these forms for requirements to reserve amenities, attend events and more. Select one or more email accounts to receive the submitted data, as well as export total results of the submissions into a .csv (excel document).

View More

Groups & Tags

Create and apply specific tags that can be used to form groups within your organization. The admin has the ability to create unlimited tags. Numerous features throughout the platform allow you to target specific groups, giving the admin the ability to email, restrict access to web pages, documents, events and much more. This feature could also be referred to as having custom access levels.

View More

Sponsorships

Your organization can collect payment to allow for businesses to sponsor the website. Sponsors can submit a logo, landing url and be provided stats specific to the number of views and clicks the ad has acquired. The admin can choose where to highlight the ad on the website for optimal viewing and opportunity for premium placement.

View More

Multi-Site Manager

Multi-Site Manager is ideal for property managers and master associations. Created specifically for the ability to manage your organizations or sub associations all from one screen. Take excel login management out of the equation, as there is no longer the need to remember the domains for each organization or store a separate username and password. All communities are one-click visibility, so you can choose which organization needs attention in real-time.

View More

Online Voting

Give members the ability to vote for the board of directors in upcoming elections. Get the votes needed to change the bylaws, covenants, ccr’s or whatever you need. The admin has full control to restrict voting to members or specific groups to gain targeted information. Choose where submissions are emailed, export the data and view aggregated results in your portal.

View More

Surveys & Polls

Similar to Online Voting. give members the ability to take quick surveys and polls. Allow the public to submit these forms or restrict to members/specific groups for data that allows your organization to make key decisions. Get instant valuable organizational feedback. The admin controls where submissions are emailed. This data can also be exported and is viewable in aggregate form.

View More

Web App

When a member is logged into the website, they can default to our new Member Portal. This will kick off the Web App user interface. From there, members can quickly decide what tasks they would like to accomplish, or information they’d like to obtain through an app-like experience on your mobile device.

Mobile App

Keep your members in the know with our mobile app and increase engagement up with community, wherever they are. They can access the latest news, chat messages, and more while on the go – with Apple or Android mobile device.

Push notifications come straight to your mobile device the moment you receive essential news and updates from the association. Choose which alerts you want to receive, and change them at any time.

Committee Planner

The committee planner tool allows the admin to create unlimited committees. The admin has the ability to assign a leader by default, as well as create unlimited positions for the committee. The assigned leader also has the ability to manage the committee. The committee can be created for members to join automatically, apply to join or setup for invitation only. By creating a committee that takes applications, this means the admin or committee leader can attach custom built forms for data collection.

View More

Event Registrations

Create events with full description and images to allow members to register. Restrict events to members or specific groups. Create ticket types and unique packages for purchase. Create daily or multi-day activities for registration and collect payment for these as well. Attach custom forms for data collection to ticket packages and activities. Allow businesses to sponsor your event and collect payment from them. This is a comprehensive feature, capable of much more, such as early bird entry fees.

View More

Amenity Reservations

Create the ability for members to reserve organization amenities. Members can choose date, time, number of guests and more for each amenity. The feature is broken into calendars so members can select times that fit their schedule. Members can also pay to reserve amenities. The admin has the option to attach custom forms for data collection about members and their guests. Similar in setup like Events Registration, this is a robust feature that allows for much more than can be listed here.

View More

ARC/Work Order

Allow members to submit info for ARC approval or work order management and have a unique ticket number assigned to the case.  Interactions between admin, approval committee and members all through the website.  Approve, reject or ask members for more information through the ticket system.  Members and admin can view these details direct on their dashboard.  Emails are used to signal notification updates and communication between parties is one click away.

View More

Sub-Admin Roles

Give admins the ability to create unique sub-admin roles by limiting access for members within the Admin Panel.

View More

Boardroom

Boardroom is a designated area where video sessions are held for meetings between the board.  This private area allows the directors/members special access to areas on the site. Boardroom also includes access to board related documents, images, private chat, web pages, as well as access to exclusive polls and voting.

Violation Tracking

Violation Tracking allows admin and members to submit violations within the community.  Whether you’re a concerned owner that see’s a streetlight out, or a member of the board that notices an owners property is in violation of a safety rule… our feature has you covered.  Submit a form, with images, deliver the violation electronically, or print and mail.  Back and forth exchange is captured in the feature until the matter is closed.
Integrations

Stripe Integration

We integrate with Stripe which allows the ability to receive your online payments within the frequency you set.  This also gives us the ability to allow you and your members to have recurring payments.  With our integration, we provide the option to pass-thru Stripe fees directly to the member.

Proptia Integration

HOA Start has partnered with Proptia to allow the ability for credentialed access, visitor management, license plate recognition, telephone entry, virtual guard kiosk and more with a mobile-first approach.  Check out their platform today.

Help Center

Success Manager

When you purchase our software, we’re dedicated to making sure all organizations have a successful roll-out to their members.  Our Success Managers are there before onboarding, helping to get you started.

They’ll start with a scheduled Zoom call to show you around the software, and prepare to place you in Onboarding.  Once you’ve went through Onboarding, they’ll circle back do a review of your website before launch, making sure you have your i’s dotted and t’s crossed.

Our Concierge services provide a full walk through for a scheduled and managed rollout, which in turn delivers the white-glove experience for a personally guided launch.

Onboarding

Once you have signed up for services, depending on what plan you choose, you’ll either be working with an Onboarding Specialist through the Support Desk or Live Zoom Sessions.  Additionally, if you opt for the White-Glove plan, your Concierge will be in charge of setting up your entire site, while you remain hands-off.

*Please note that after your website is launched, you will still have access to your Concierge for support.  They will assist you in all needs EXCEPT the support and managing of the organization members, for items such as:  Approval of Registrations, Approval of Reservations, Recording of Payments, Managing of Violations/Ticketing, Etc.

The Concierge will be there to upload documents, create new custom forms, new web pages, and dedicated Live Session support.
